BUTCHEROV

With over 170 years combined butchery experience, Butcherov Butchers is a leading supplier of the highest-quality meat products in Bad Rappenau.
Working hard to better the standard of any butchers and caterers. Butcherov Butchers boasts a state of the art temperature controlled cutting room and full HACCP and traceability systems.
